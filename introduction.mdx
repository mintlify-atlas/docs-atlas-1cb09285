## Learning Paths by Level

Our platform offers three specialized learning tracks based on your English proficiency:

### Gramática (Inicial)
Focus on basic and advanced language structure, real-world phrase breakdown, and text composition. Perfect for beginners building their foundation.

### Auditivo (Intermedio)
Emphasize listening comprehension, understanding movies and conversations, and developing reading skills. Ideal for intermediate learners improving fluency.

### Conversacional (Avanzado)
Master spoken fluency, oral expression with common contractions, and natural conversation flow. Designed for advanced speakers seeking native-level proficiency.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign in with Google or GitHub using NextAuth authentication. Your profile is created automatically with secure OAuth integration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Set your English level (inicial, básico, intermedio, avanzado), location, phone number, and learning preferences.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Book Your First Class">
    Browse available time slots in the calendar, select individual or group class, define your learning focus, and complete payment via Mercado Pago.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Attend & Practice">
    Join classes via Google Meet 60 minutes before start time using your unique access code. Complete AI-generated activities after each session.
  </Step>
</Steps>
