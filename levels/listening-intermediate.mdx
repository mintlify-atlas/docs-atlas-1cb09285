The Listening level is designed for intermediate learners who have a solid grammatical foundation and are ready to focus on understanding spoken English and improving reading comprehension.

## Overview

<Card title="Learning Focus" icon="headphones">
  **Auditivo (Intermedio)** - The intermediate level concentrates on auditory comprehension, understanding movies and conversations, and developing strong reading skills.
</Card>

## What You'll Learn

The Listening level emphasizes three core areas:

### 1. Auditory Comprehension

Train your ear to understand spoken English in various contexts:

- **Natural Speech Patterns**: Understanding how native speakers actually talk
- **Different Accents**: Exposure to American, British, and other English accents
- **Speed Variations**: Practice with slow, normal, and fast-paced speech
- **Informal Language**: Comprehending casual conversations and colloquialisms

### 2. Media Understanding

Learn to comprehend English through authentic media:

- **Movie Analysis**: Understanding dialogue, plot, and context from English films
- **Conversation Practice**: Following and participating in real-time discussions
- **Podcast Listening**: Comprehending longer-form audio content
- **Video Content**: Understanding various video formats (news, interviews, tutorials)

### 3. Reading Capacity

Strengthen your ability to read and understand written English:

- **Reading Speed**: Improve your reading pace without sacrificing comprehension
- **Vocabulary Expansion**: Learn new words in context through extensive reading
- **Text Analysis**: Understanding main ideas, supporting details, and inference
- **Different Genres**: Exposure to news articles, stories, essays, and informal writing

## Class Format

Listening level classes follow this structure:

1. **Listening Exercise (30 minutes)**: Audio or video content analysis
2. **Discussion (25 minutes)**: Group conversation about the content
3. **Reading Practice (25 minutes)**: Text comprehension and discussion
4. **Recap & Preview (10 minutes)**: Summary and next class preview

## Success Tips

<Info>
Your brain needs time to adjust to processing English audio. Don't get discouraged if you don't understand everything at first - comprehension improves rapidly with consistent practice.
</Info>

To succeed at the Listening level:

1. **Immerse Daily**: Listen to English for at least 30 minutes every day (podcasts, music, videos)
2. **Use Subtitles Wisely**: Start with English subtitles, not your native language
3. **Read Extensively**: Read English content that interests you for 20+ minutes daily
4. **Practice Active Listening**: Focus fully on what you're hearing, don't just have it in the background
5. **Accept Imperfection**: You don't need to understand every word to comprehend the message

## Recommended Practice Resources

Between classes, practice with these types of content:

- **Podcasts**: Choose topics you enjoy to stay motivated
- **YouTube Channels**: Educational content in English on your hobbies
- **English News**: Short news articles or video news clips
- **Movies/Series**: Start with familiar content you've seen before
- **Audiobooks**: Try simple stories or books you've already read
